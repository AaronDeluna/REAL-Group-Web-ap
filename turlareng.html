What you need to know before visiting Turkey

Turkey is a very attractive destination for vacation. It is famous for its rich history, natural beauties, diverse cultures and delicious cuisine. Here are some attractive features of Turkey for vacationers:

Historical Riches: Turkey is steeped in thousands of years of history. There are historical sites such as Hagia Sophia, Topkapi Palace and the Grand Bazaar in Istanbul; fairy chimneys and historic underground cities in Cappadocia; and many important ancient cities such as the ancient theater and the Temple of Artemis in Ephesus.

Magnificent Natural Beauties: Turkey offers a variety of natural beauty. Fethiye with its turquoise sea in Oludeniz and the natural beauty of the Beydağ Mountains; the white travertine terraces of Pamukkale; the beautiful beaches of Antalya and the Taurus Mountains; the fairy chimneys and underground cities of Cappadocia are just a few of them.
